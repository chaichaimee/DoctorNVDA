## Professional Care for Your Screen Reader
Is your NVDA feeling sluggish? Are strange errors popping up after installing new add-ons? **DoctorNVDA** acts as a personal physician for your software. It provides high-precision tools to diagnose conflicts and restore health instantly.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Main Menu Shortcut:** Press **Alt + Windows + D**

## Detailed Feature Guide

### • Create & Restore NVDA Setting
Your settings are precious. This feature creates a **"Point-in-Time"** recovery of your perfect configuration.

> **The Safety Workflow:**
> 1. **Create Recovery:** Save your current stable settings.
> 2. **The Problem:** If your configuration becomes unstable or speech dictionaries get corrupted.
> 3. **Instant Restore:** Simply pick your desired recovery date, and DoctorNVDA will roll back all core files automatically.

### • Binary Search Debugging (The Add-on Detective)
When you have dozens of add-ons, finding the one causing a conflict is like finding a needle in a haystack. DoctorNVDA uses an automated **Binary Search** process to find it for you.

### • Check RAM
Instantly retrieve precise details about your system's memory configuration (Manufacturer, Capacity, Type, Speed) and copy it to the clipboard.

> [!TIP]
> **Doctor's Order:** Always create a recovery point before installing major new add-ons or making significant changes to your configuration.
